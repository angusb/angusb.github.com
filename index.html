Hi.

My name is Angus Burton.

In my spare time I enjoy photography, reading, hacking on side projects (I just bought a Raspberry Pi!), and spending time with friends and family. I also have a soft spot for good food and HBO.

I think writing is important, and I plan to do that here. Feel free to follow me on Twitter @angusnb or shoot me an email.
